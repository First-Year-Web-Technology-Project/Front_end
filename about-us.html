Ceylon Nutmeg

Celebrated for its warm and musky flavour and sweet fragrance, Ceylon Nutmeg is a versatile spice that delivers a wide range of flavour notes depending on how it is used and the presence or absence of the shell

Ceylon Cardamom

Known for its light green pod colour and unique flavour, Ceylon Cardamom from Tropical Island is locally grown and naturally processed to preserve its goodness.

Mace

Made from the delicate outer cover of Ceylon Nutmeg, the blades of Mace are removed by hand and slowly sun-dried, enhancing its bitter citrus and floral notes. Tropical Island Mace has a unique flavour that is aromatic, and spicy, yet pungent.
