The delivery time depends on your location and the shipping method chosen. Typically, orders are processed within 1-3 business days, and shipping times range from 3-7 days domestically and 7-14 days internationally.

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Please note that items must be in their original condition with tags attached.
